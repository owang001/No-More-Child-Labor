About Us and Our Mission

We are committed to helping wounded kids find their place.

The Website is created in 2025 by student of Himalayan University decided to serve or give values back to society. Inspired by analyzing the current situation of the weaker section of society, from 1947 to till now we are facing poverty in our country and childrens are the most effected victims of this unstable society.

We always wanted to help the baggers but we can't help by paying 10-20 rupees this will not solve the problem of our society. We all will have to stand together to help the Child baggers. It is not always the responsibility of government to raise hand for everyone. Non-government Organizations are doing exceptionally great to help the society but child baggers are the only one who are most effected victim. We target colleges, students, Teachers, and faculties to come together and pay whatever they can. We have not putted any bar for payment. We will collectively use all the money with the help of NGO's to properly support the Child baggers society.

Through relevant educational, innovative healthcare and market oriented employment programs, We aims to promote the empowerment of disadvantaged child baggers, youth and women. The Foundation shall implement the highest possible methodology and technology in order to achieve an ideal Smilesocial SROI, return on investments for implementing good governance as well as promote good governance. To link business competitiveness of the corporate with social development initiatives; also to sensitize privileged children, youth and citizens in general to promote Civic Driven Change.
